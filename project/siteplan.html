Site Name

The Giant's Factory

I chose this site name because it is generally a catchphrase used by people who practice bodybuilding and it is very catchy.

Site Purpose

My website aims to give tips on how to do better gym exercises, as well as some precautions that should be taken. The website will provide a search/selesction button option to bring up the different exercises based on the workout type. Workout A (biceps and back), B (complete lower body) and C (chest and shoulders).

Scenarios

How many times could I train per week?

How long and at what pace should I train and do these exercises?

What should my diet be like before and after training?

Color Schema

  • #f0f0f2: I will use it as the main background.
  • #90DA00: I will use it for buttons or any call to action on the page.
  • #B80C09: It will be used in important elements and close to the images on the website.
  • #08375B: It will be used for my menu, titles and footer.

Typography

Bebas Neue: I will use it in titles and striking texts.

Open Sans: I will use it in paragraphs and descriptions, in the navigation menu and in image captions.
